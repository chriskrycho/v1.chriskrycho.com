Look: we all know that cool URIs don’t change. But we also know that sometimes, you have a website full of the things you wrote when you were 18–23 years old, and that none of that needs to live on in the public eye. The things which once lived here? They fit in that latter bucket. (I should know: I read back through them, God help me.)

If you really want to dig into what 19-year-old Chris Krycho was like—the kinds of things he (that is: I) published to Xanga in 2005–2006—the content is on GitHub and I haven’t hidden it. But it’s there only as a historical artifact, and I make no promises not to fully remove it at some point.

— Chris Krycho, January 2023
